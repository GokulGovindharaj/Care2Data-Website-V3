The Intelligence Layer for Clinical Data

Clinical research generates vast volumes of complex data across trials, analytics systems, and regulatory submissions. Yet much of this data remains fragmented across platforms and workflows, making it difficult to maintain clarity, traceability, and regulatory confidence.

About Us

Care2Data was founded to address this challenge.

By combining deep life sciences expertise with structured knowledge modeling, Care2Data transforms complex clinical datasets into governed knowledge systems. Through ontology frameworks, semantic modeling, and intelligent validation approaches, we help organizations bring structure, context, and reliability to clinical data workflows in highly regulated environments.

Our work focuses on enabling life sciences organizations to move beyond fragmented datasets toward connected, trusted knowledge systems.

Our Purpose

To bring clarity, trust, and intelligence to clinical data—so life sciences organizations can make confident decisions and deliver better medicines faster.

Care2Data believes that clinical data quality is not simply a technical requirement but a foundational element of scientific integrity. By structuring and governing data across the clinical research lifecycle, we help organizations transform fragmented information into reliable knowledge that supports regulatory confidence and better healthcare outcomes.

Who We Are

Clinical Standards & Compliance

Built for Regulatory-Grade Clinical Data Validation

Care2Data frameworks align with globally recognized clinical data standards, regulatory expectations, and data integrity principles.

By embedding these standards into validation and knowledge systems, we help organizations ensure clinical datasets remain traceable, defensible, and submission-ready.

CDISC Standards

Support for validation workflows aligned with CDISC standards such as SDTM, ensuring consistent and structured datasets for regulatory submissions.

FDA Regulatory Expectations

Frameworks designed to help teams verify analysis outputs and ensure submission-ready datasets before regulatory review.

ALCOA++ Data Integrity Principles

Ensuring data remains Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate, along with Complete, Consistent, Enduring, and Available.

GAMP5 Principles

Validation frameworks aligned with GAMP5 guidance for regulated systems, ensuring traceability, governance, and methodological reliability.
