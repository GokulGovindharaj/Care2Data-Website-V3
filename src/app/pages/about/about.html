Our Foundation

Ensuring accuracy, traceability, and compliance remains a core clinical challenge

To bring clarity, trust, and intelligence to clinical data—so life sciences organizations can make confident decisions and deliver better medicines faster.

Care2Data was founded to address this challenge. By combining deep life sciences expertise with structured knowledge modeling, Care2Data transforms complex clinical datasets into governed knowledge systems. Through ontology frameworks, semantic modeling, and intelligent validation approaches, we help organizations bring structure, context, and reliability to clinical data workflows in highly regulated environments.

Care2Data believes that clinical data quality is not simply a technical requirement but a foundational element of scientific integrity. By structuring and governing data across the clinical research lifecycle, we help organizations transform fragmented information into reliable knowledge that supports regulatory confidence and better healthcare outcomes.

Our team works at the intersection of clinical research, data standards, and knowledge engineering. Rather than treating data as isolated technical artifacts, we design systems that capture the meaning, relationships, and context behind clinical datasets—enabling organizations to operate with greater clarity and confidence.

What Distinguishes Us

What Distinguishes Our Approach

Care2Data follows a validation-first philosophy. While many data systems prioritize automation alone, our approach begins with governance, traceability, and audit readiness—ensuring validation workflows remain structured, explainable (XAI-enabled), and aligned with evolving regulatory expectations.

Our approach emphasizes:

01

Governance-first design

Ensuring validation processes support transparency, traceability, and regulatory compliance

02

Semantic intelligence

Using ontology frameworks to connect datasets through meaning and relationships rather than isolated rules

03

Structured validation frameworks

Enabling reproducible, explainable (XAI-driven), and audit-ready validation processes across the clinical data lifecycle

04

Integrated validation and verification

Combining ontology-driven validation with graph-based verification models to identify inconsistencies, confirm data integrity, and ensure completeness across datasets

05

Operational integration

Embedding governed intelligence within existing clinical workflows without replacing established systems

Regulatory Alignment

Clinical Standards & Compliance

Built for Regulatory-Grade Clinical Data Validation. Care2Data frameworks align with globally recognized clinical data standards, regulatory expectations, and data integrity principles. By embedding these standards into validation and knowledge systems, we help organizations ensure clinical datasets remain traceable, defensible, and submission-ready.

CDISC Standards

Support for validation workflows aligned with CDISC standards such as SDTM, ensuring consistent and structured datasets for regulatory submissions

FDA Regulatory Expectations

Frameworks designed to help teams verify analysis outputs and ensure submission-ready datasets before regulatory review

ALCOA++ Data Integrity Principles

Ensuring data remains Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate, along with Complete, Consistent, Enduring, and Available

GAMP5 Principles

Validation frameworks aligned with GAMP5 guidance for regulated systems, ensuring traceability, governance, and methodological reliability

Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To accelerate drug discovery and enable the delivery of high-quality medicines earlier by becoming the most trusted global clinical research technology partner. We pursue this vision by enabling fully validated, intelligent systems that strengthen the integrity and reliability of data throughout the clinical research lifecycle.
